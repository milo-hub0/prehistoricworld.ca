What to ExpectHandcrafted Statues: The park is a decades-long passion project created by two brothers, Paul and Serge Dupuis, who constructed the concrete models by hand.Educational Trail: The flat, stroller-friendly path takes about 1 to 1.5 hours to walk. Each exhibit features an informational plaque in both English and French.Amenities: There is a large sandbox for kids to act like paleontologists and dig for fossil replicas, alongside an open grass area with picnic tables. No food or concessions are sold on-site, so visitors must pack their own lunch.
